The Database has a simple layoout with a single table. (see image for layout)

user_id is the primary key and is autogenerated when a new row is created.
user_email is the email of the user that they subbmitted from the site.
need_meme is a single binary digit (1 or 0) 1 indicating a email needs to be sent and 0 indicating they don't need a email being sent.
